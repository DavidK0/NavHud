Current features:
- 4 grid modes (CCE, CCI, ENU, LVLH)
- 3 velocity modes (Surface, Orbit, Target)
- attitude/antiattitude indicator
- target/antitarget indicators when a target is selected
- togglable zoom-based or fixed size for the grids and attitude indicator
- Prograde/retrograde, normal/antinormal, radial in/out for orbital velocity
- per-save config files, saved in `saves/`
- line thickness/symbol scaling
- 3d rendezvous trajectories
- target tracking
